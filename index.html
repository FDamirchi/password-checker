    HIBP privacy (important)

    • Your password is hashed in your browser using SHA-1.
    • Only the first 5 hex characters of that hash are sent to HIBP (k-anonymity).
    • HIBP returns many suffixes; we match yours locally.
    • Result: HIBP never receives your full hash or password.
